You will be working with the sales team in a number of different ways. Mainly helping with targeting and reaching out to potential clients and assisting in developing marketing strategies to promote the companies' services. The main responsibilities that you will be involved with are as follows:

Brand Relations Intern

Application Deadline Application Review Basis Compensation Duration Commitment Days
Open On Rolling Basis College Credit Stipend Flex. Internship Period 1-3 days per week

Application Requirements

  • Must be proficient in Microsoft office applications, including Excel
  • Must have an interest in brand and retailer relations

Position Description

Assist in the execution of outreach efforts to potential partners and maintain up to date records of their status. You will also assist in developing marketing strategies for promoting the companies' services. Specifically, you will be helping with the following:

  • Coordinate and provide outreach support related to the marketing/brand relations department.
  • General administrative support to assist in the execution of all aspects of project management.
  • Work closely with the Brand Relations Manager.
  • Conduct market research and competitor analysis.

  • Work independently on specifically appointed special projects.
