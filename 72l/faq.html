Q. What does “Powered by 72Lux” mean? Who is 72Lux?

A. 72Lux is a technology company that allows you to easily purchase products you see on our website without having to redirect you to several different websites. 72Lux’s technology lets you check out from many different reputable retailers with a single checkout process on our website.

Q. How do I make a purchase?

A. We’re partnering with 72Lux to make it easy for you to purchase the products we feature and curate on our website. Once you have found an item that you would like to purchase, simply click on the button that says “Add to Bag.” This will take you to a shopping bag confirmation page. On this page, click the button that says, “Checkout.” From there all you need to do is fill out your shipping, billing and payment information and then click “Place Order”. If you have any problems with this at all please contact CustomerCare@72Lux.com or click on the “Questions?” tab on the bottom of the checkout page to chat with a member of our Customer Care Team and we will be happy to assist you.

Q. Which retailers are your products from?

A. We partner with 72Lux and a number of reputable, authorized retailers to provide you with a seamless user experience entirely within our website. All retailers are specified on the product page so you can know where the product is shipping from prior to purchasing it. They will also be specified on the checkout page and in the order confirmation email we send you after you have submitted your order.

Q. What payment methods do you accept?

A. We accept American Express, Visa, Discover and Mastercard.

Q. How do I track my order?

A. Each retailer will send you an order confirmation email followed by an email with the tracking information. The system usually takes up to 48 hours to generate the tracking code for you. If you have any questions about the code please contact the retailer directly and reference the order number in the confirmation email.

Q. What is your return policy?

A. All returns will go through the retailer you purchased your product(s) from. The retailer shipping to you is specified in the order confirmation email. For specific details please review the return policy on the retailer’s website or for more information regarding a specific retailer please contact us at CustomerCare@72Lux.com and we’d be more than happy to help.

Q. How do I cancel an order?

A. Wait until the order is processed and then contact the respective retailers from your Order Confirmation email.

Q. How do I change a color or size in my order?

A. Wait until the order is processed and then contact the respective retailers from your Order Confirmation email as soon as you receive it.

Q. Who do I contact if I have questions BEFORE I place an order that is not listed above?

A. Send an email to CustomerCare@72Lux.com with your question(s) or chat with us by clicking the “Questions?” tab at the bottom of the checkout page.

Q. What if I have questions AFTER I place an order that is not listed above?

A. Please contact the corresponding retailer with your question and order confirmation number.
