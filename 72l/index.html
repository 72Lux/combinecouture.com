How it works

Shoppable® by 72Lux enables websites to sell featured products. This ecommerce platform and universal checkout are the optimal way to monetize yoru content and increase engagement.

1

Log on to shoppable.co and register your website.

2

Choose previously integrated products or add new merchandise.

3

Select how you want to display Shoppable® items within your article.

4

Launch on your site by pasting the code generated into your CMS.

5

Start selling!
