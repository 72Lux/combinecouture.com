SHOPPABLETM PLATFORM

72Lux's technology, Shoppable, enables digital publishers to sell the products featured within your editorial. This ecommerce platform solution is the optimal way to monetize your content.

72Lux makes the process of incorporating shopping functionality easy and inexpensive, leading to quick profitability from a new revenue stream. Visitors can purchase the looks they see in your content without ever leaving your site.

The opportunity leverages technology that enables customers—your visitors—to buy from multiple retailers with only one check-out interface. This interface is customized and branded by your website.

There are multiple options for integration depending on your site, your needs, and your vision for the retail possibilities.
