HotHANDBAGS

Handbags are the most desirable fashion accessory for women from every age group and every class. Apart from carrying personal items, it is a complimenting add-on, which gives a stylish look to your personality. They come in variety of designs, sizes and shapes. On different occasions, handbags play an important role in your look. Handling the wrong kind of bag on a meticulous occasion may distort your appearance and shift the fashion statement that you were aiming for.

For the environmentally conscious fashionista, considering choosing an eco-friendly tote that doesn’t completely throw off your entire ensemble can get a little rough. Rocking a gorgeous pair of Louboutin’s doesn’t exactly mesh well with a Trader Joes canvas tote that has a beach ball and some fish on it. Needless to say, I’d like to rescue you from this probable spectacle.

At the very least, these bags redefine the idea of your basic summer tote. To be frank, I’d be utterly surprised if you didn’t get looks of jealousy shooting your way from now until end of August. So before you go to lug your Memorial Day Weekend basics in a recyclable fashion catastrophe, check out one of these marvelous go-green bags that are not only perfect substitutions to whatever it is you have now, but they are hip and tres, tres chic, haven’t you heard?
